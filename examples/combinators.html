Child combinator: .menu > .row

Alpha
Beta
Gamma

Alpha / Beta / Gamma form a flex row (their .row is a direct child of .menu).

Delta
Epsilon

Delta / Epsilon do NOT form a row: their .row is a grandchild of .menu, so .menu > .row does not match and they stack.
