z-index reorder test

Three overlapping absolute boxes, in document order: blue (z=1), green (z=10), orange (z=5). Stage 2 sorts them by z-index ascending: blue first, then orange, then green on top. Without Stage 2, they would paint in document order (blue at the back, green in the middle, orange on top) and the result would be visibly wrong.

z=1 blue
z=10 green
z=5 orange
green (z=10) should be ON TOP of orange (z=5) which is on top of blue (z=1)
